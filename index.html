OUR STORY 1ST CLAN ROADMAP OUR TEAM HOW TO FAQ

A new generation of dwarves

is making its way into the Blockchain.

Their lost treasure is waiting for you !

For centuries our tribe, composed of 3 clans, has been living in the Sif mountain. One day, while we were casually mining gold, our brother Eitri made a wrong move with his pickaxe. Suddenly, a warp hole appeared and we landed in the Blockchain… Digitized in NFTs. But our tribe got separated, and our clans were split into 3 different locations.

We then realized that an ancient prophecy that foretold the demise of the Old World and promised untold treasures even greater than gold… had just come true. The new wealth is no longer extracted from stone, but from the Blockchain.

Ready to come feast with us?

A collection of unique NFTs

to find the dwarves’ lost treasure.

The Rich Dwarves Tribe is a collection of unique and rare digital artworks, stored as ERC-721 Tokens on the Ethereum Blockchain, and hosted on IPFS. This first chapter, MO$$O’s clan, composed of 4499 dwarves distributed into four divisions. The dwarves have all been digitized in high quality 3D NFTs.

However, they were not born yesterday. They are particularly suspicious of anyone who’s after their wealth. That’s why the content of their treasure won’t be revealed here.

Join the tribe to find out more !

MO$$O’s family

The legendary MO$$O

Him? He’s the big boss. He takes the decisions for the whole clan. He is the wealthiest of his clan. And yet, he is just one of the Dwarf King’s sons.
He only has access to a fraction of the tremendous treasure of the Rich Dwarves Tribe. I’ll let you imagine the value of the whole tribe’s treasure. It’s beyond your wildest dreams.

The Guardians

They are the boss’s personal guard. They protect his treasure. In exchange, MO$$O gives them all a huge income to ensure their loyalty.
The guardians are the richest dwarves after the legendary MO$$O.

The counselors

They are part of the council and take important decisions for the clan. They work directly with the Guardians and relay MO$$O’s orders to the entire clan. Their common goal is to increase the clan’s wealth.

The Conveyors

They form the largest part of the clan. It is thanks to them that the dwarves’ treasure has been growing all this time. Their ancestral knowhow in mining allows them to
be very efficient in the Blockchain.

Even in the crypto world,

a dwarf is still a dwarf, they adapted quickly…

Keeper

They are always looking for
anything of value. However, they are unwilling to part with their fortune. They love to stack more and more wealth and their worst nightmare is to imagine a stranger stealing it. Blockchain offers them the greatest opportunity of their life, because it is an endless mine.

Cautious

The dwarves never had an easy
life. From the beginning, they had to live in hidden places to protect themselves from the constant threats of the outside. Since then, they have been suspicious of anyone who tries to approach them. That’s why their new life in the ethereum blockchain suits them perfectly.

Hardworkers

If dwarves have one quality, this is it. They don’t mind working hard, as long as it’s worth it. True to their nature, they are able to find out the good plans before anyone else. However, there’s no need to try to get them to work for free !

Partygoers

Who doesn’t like a drink once the job is done ? The dwarves can’t imagine not going to the tavern to have a good beer, homebrewed by their peers. They are able to go from the Blockchain to the tavern within a snap of their fingers to get a drink and chat with their tribe.

Combative

Continuously fighting against those who are looking for trouble, our dwarves 3.0 are formidable fighters. They stand up for their tribe and will never let their brothers down. They traded in their pickaxes and mine wagons for the latests computers and graphics cards that would make Satoshi Nakamoto blush !

Community

Another quality of dwarves is their sense of family. Touch a hair of their mates and a whole pack of enraged dwarves will pounce on you. Everything they collect is shared and goes into the tribe’s war chest.

NEW: they’re also drippin’!

their arrival into the crypto world has not changed their taste for luxury. As long as they are stuck in the blockchain, they might as well make some cash ! They mine crypto like crazy and don’t stop until late in the day. One thing is for sure: with them, you’ll understand what it means to win big. Join them now!

Roadmap.

Welcome to the «Rich Dwarves Tribe».

As you will discover below, our goal is to build a tribe that brings together the most passionate collectors and investors.

1. Our Goal

Our top priority is to deliver high quality NFTs for the community. We have chosen to work with artists who, like the dwarves and the rest of the team, works from the depths of the mine. This keeps us focused on our main goal : to deliver high quality NFTs. Each dwarf follows the same creative process with high standards. So you can enjoy and flex on social media your digital artworks and watch them appreciate in value.

2. Our Vision

Our years of hard work allowed us to grow several successful businesses in sectors such as e-commerce, real estate and crypto investments. We realized that Blockchain technology would change the world as we know it. Our mindset combined with the fact that we all started our first businesses in our teenage years allowed us to evolve as fast as the world around us these last 10 years. The world is changing and this is just the beginning. Our goal is to bring together in the same universe a tribe of discreet hard workers and genius
entrepreneurs who will contribute to change the world. Who better than dwarves to represent us? This NFT project is not just another tick off the list of what we have already accomplished. It’s the result of years of work developing skills and building a team of enthusiasts. The « Rich Dwarves Tribe » could not be more representative of who we really are on a daily basis. We unite our skills with a common goal: to imagine, create, develop and undertake with those who are like us.

3. The Community

Without you, nothing is possible. Despite the fact that there is a hierarchy in the dwarves tribe, we wanted everyone to be on the same level. The « Rich Dwarves Tribe » isn’t just a collection of high quality artistic NFTs. It is also and above all a useful project that brings together investors, entrepreneurs and celebrities who shares the same values of fellowship with a common goal. That’s why, owning at least one NFT from the « Rich Dwarves Tribe » automatically grants you a spot in our tribe. So we can go big together.

4. The Rewards

This first chapter is about MO$$O’s clan. Once all the dwarves of the clan reunited we’ll be able to retrieve the clan’s great treasure. Loads of rewards will also be distributed to the most committed tribe’s members. Each new chapter will progressively reveal new treasures of
ever-increasing value. We’re not talking about mugs or t-shirts, but « treasures ». Some of them might change your life (the rewards will be revealed when we hit 30k members on the Discord). That’s why this project is only for collectors and investors who like to see their efforts rewarded.

5. The Future

Each clan has a treasure that can be retrieved in the real world. How can you take part in the adventure ? Once you’ll own at least one Rich Dwarf from the first clan, you will be able to join the second chapter. You will then be among genuine collectors and investors who will have perceived the true value of the Rich Dwarves Tribe. We believe that top tier entrepreneurship is art in its own form. This is why this project’s goal is to bring together a community of passionate entrepreneurs, collectors, investors and celebrities around a common artistic project. We made the choice to use 100% of the royalties for the growth of the project and to increase the value of the rewards.

6. The Metaverse

Once all the clans are reunited and their great wealth retrieved, the tribe will be able to celebrate at the tavern in The Sandbox or Decentraland (we’ll let you decide). Beyond having a place to meet, we really want to enter the future by investing in the creation of new projects where each of your NFTs will have its use, a stacking utility will be developed, dwarves are well known miners so each NFT will mine coins for you. An incubator will be created in the Metaverse to bring together the new generation of investors, entrepreneurs and celebrities. This will allow us to unite around common values. You will be able to develop your projects with a dedicated fund and take part in the development of new ones.
Because together we go further, join the new generation of dwarves
